About iBot AI

What Can iBot AI Do?

  • Answer questions from a built-in knowledge base or fetch info from Wikipedia and web searches.
  • Provide weather updates for any city (as long as it’s not on Mars).
  • Perform math calculations faster than you can say "algebra."
  • Tell jokes, sing songs like "Happy Birthday," or open YouTube for your favorite tunes.
  • Support voice input and output for a hands-free experience (if your browser plays nice).

User Commands :

  • Tell me about [ YOUR FAVOURITE THING ]. For example : Tell me about solar system.
  • Who is ... , What is ... ,How is ... type questions.
  • calculate [ ANY NUMBERS ]. For example : calculate 7+6-5/4 . or calculate sin,cos,tan problems. ex : sin(5).
  • Date and Time .
  • Play [ YOUR FAVOURITE SONGS OR VIDEOS ]. For example : play anirudh songs . it will open the youtube and give the many option and the user click to play songs.
  • General Knowledge questions.
